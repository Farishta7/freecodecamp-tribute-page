Michael Jackson

Celebrating the Iconic Legacy

Here's a time line of Michael Jackson's life:

  • 1958 - Born on August 29, 1958, in Gary, Indiana, USA, Michael Joseph Jackson was the seventh of nine children in the Jackson family, destined for a remarkable career in music and entertainment.
  • 1969 - At the age of 11, Michael Jackson and his siblings formed the Jackson 5 and signed a record deal with Motown Records, propelling him into the music industry as a child star.
  • 1979 - Michael released his first solo album as an adult, "Off the Wall," which marked his transition from child prodigy to a successful adult artist.
  • 1982 - The release of the groundbreaking album "Thriller," which became the best-selling album of all time, earned multiple Grammy Awards, and established him as the "King of Pop."
  • 1983 - Michael debuted the iconic moonwalk dance move during a performance of "Billie Jean" on the television special "Motown 25: Yesterday, Today, Forever."
  • 1984 - A record-breaking year at the Grammy Awards, where he won eight awards in a single night
  • 1994 - A significant moment in his personal life, Michael married Lisa Marie Presley, daughter of Elvis Presley, in a high-profile celebrity wedding.
  • 2009 - Tragically, on June 25, 2009, Michael Jackson passed away at the age of 50, leaving behind a lasting legacy in the world of music and entertainment, and marking a deeply mourned moment in pop culture history.
